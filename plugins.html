Bukkit Plugins

Well, I should've explained that I also make Bukkit Plugins on the front page. If you don't know what Bukkit is, you can go check them out here. It's a method of modding the minecraft server platform, and the possibilities are endless. It's a very big API, and it how minecraft server networks usually achieve minigames and other cool stuff like that.

I for one make plugins that may be useful for server owners and players alike. They're very small, and add little features that may make your day better while playing the game. Here they are!




the plugins

ItemHelper is a simple plugin that helps you when map-making or setting up a spawn area, etc. You can custom name and lore your items, fill a dispenser or chest with the item you are holding, and things like that. Download it here.

CrackZombies was created when my friend said he wished zombies were more powerful in Minecraft. I offered to make him a simple plugin that fulfulled his needs and I have to say I'm proud of it. Download it here.
